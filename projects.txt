Title: CO2 Sensor on CH32v003 (10 cent MCU!!)
Number: 6 
- Used a CH32v003F4P6 on a custom PCB 
- 2 SK6812 for communicating CO2 levels
- The CH32v003 has a *single* wire debug, no SWCLK! 
- Manufactered by JLCPCB 
- Coded using CH32fun framework https://github.com/cnlohr/ch32fun
- Chip size / some info: https://w.willx86.com/2025/11/05/ch32v003-on-platform-io.html
- A picture of the board ( at the bottom !) https://w.willx86.com/2025/11/05/custom-stm32-board.html
